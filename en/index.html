Familoo — Shared Family Calendar

The family calendar for everyone, from 9 to 99 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Organize family life with ease. Share your events, sync your schedules and keep everyone in the loop — no complications, no tracking, no hassle.

✨ Smart Features

🎯

Automatic Emojis

Type "Dentist" and the 🦷 emoji appears automatically. Over 1,200 words recognized in 9 languages for visual events.

🎨

Smart Colors

Events automatically color themselves by type: medical in red, sports in green, school in blue... Readable at a glance.

👤

Member Assignment

Who's taking the kids to soccer? Assign each event to one or more family members.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

One Family Account

Only the creator needs an account (Google, Apple or email). Others join via QR code — no sign-up required.

📥

Calendar Import

Import events from Google Calendar, Cozi, or any ICS calendar. Easy transition, zero re-entry.

💎

Family Supporter

One Supporter purchase and the whole family benefits: no ads, custom themes, exclusive sounds.

🔔

Custom Notifications

Configurable reminders for each event. Choose when and how to be notified.

🌍

9 Languages Available

French, English, German, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch and more. Each member uses the app in their language.

☁️

Instant Sync

All devices synced in real-time. Add an event, everyone sees it immediately.
